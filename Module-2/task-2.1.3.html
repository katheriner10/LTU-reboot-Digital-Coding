Frankenstein

Frankenstein is a novel originally written by Mary Shelly.

It has been adapted for stage.

Danny Boyle and Nick Dear created one of the most famous adaptations

It was a very successful production.

Success was down to many factors

The production cast two very famous performers which boosted box office sales

Danny Boyle is also a very well known director and this created a lot of buzz around the production.

Recording

The production was recorded.

Response

The production recieved both critical and public acclaim.

Future productions
